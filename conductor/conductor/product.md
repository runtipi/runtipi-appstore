# Product Guide

## Initial Concept
The Runtipi App Store is the official repository for applications available for download on [Runtipi](https://github.com/runtipi/runtipi). It serves as a centralized hub for managing, validating, and distributing application configurations (Docker Compose, metadata, etc.) to users of the Runtipi OS.

## Vision & Goals
### Target Users
- **Self-Hoster Enthusiasts:** Individuals who host their own services and seek a curated list of applications.
- **Runtipi OS Users:** Users who want a simple, one-click installation experience for their self-hosted apps.

### Core Goals
- **Diversity and Accessibility:** Provide a wide variety of easy-to-install apps for diverse use cases.
- **Quality and Reliability:** Ensure all apps are secure, performant, and correctly configured.
- **Ease of Contribution:** Make it seamless for developers to contribute new apps or bug fixes to existing ones.

## Key Features
- **Validation Scripts:** Automated checks (using AJV and custom scripts) to ensure app configurations adhere to the required schema.
- **Unified Metadata Schema:** A standardized structure for all application metadata to ensure consistency across the store.
